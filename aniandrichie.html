Are the ceremony and reception at the same venue?
Please note that the ceremony will be held at The Mural Room inside the Santa Barbara Courthouse. Dinner and dancing will follow at the The Good Bar inside the Kimpton Goodland Hotel.


What time should I arrive?
The ceremony will begin at 3pm on July 13th. We recommend guests arrive 15-30 minutes prior to explore the beautiful Santa Barbara Courthouse. Following the ceremony, we ask you join us at The Good Bar at 5:30pm for cocktail hour.


What's the parking situation?
Ceremony: There is a City lot on Anacapa Street across from the courthouse (first 75 minutes free) as well as street parking around the Courthouse.
Reception: There is plentiful, free parking at The Goodland Hotel.


What does festive and happy attire even mean??
Have fun with your outfit, feel free to be creative. There's no strict dress code- anything from super goth to chic couture.


Can I bring a date?
Although we would love to celebrate with everyone, we have a strict guest list to stay on budget. We will only be able to accommodate those listed on your invitation.
